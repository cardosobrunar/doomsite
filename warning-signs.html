Warning Signs

Are you constantly tilted to the point where you're graying at 25? You're not alone. League of Legends has taken many Summoners on a wild emotional ride. But you don’t have to go through it alone — we're here to help.

  • Neglecting your personal relationships
  • Calling out of work to go on a 14-game loss streak
  • Difficulty sleeping and dreaming about gameplay
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling more than once
  • Letting in-game rage affect real life
  • Physical symptoms like nausea, tension headaches, and overheating

Take care of yourself. There’s more to life than LP.
