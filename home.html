Welcome to the League of Legends Support Site!

League of Legends can be an intense game that affects your mental health. If you’re feeling overwhelmed, you're not alone. We are here to help.

If you are experiencing negative side effects from your gaming experience, it’s important to seek help. Our website provides options for support, including resources and tips to manage your gameplay habits.
