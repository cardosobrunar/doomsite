Alternative Options

Sometimes, taking a break is the bravest move you can make. Here are some powerful tools and resources to support you outside the Rift.

Professional Help

  • BetterHelp - Online Therapy
  • 7 Cups - Free Emotional Support
  • Crisis Text Line - Text HOME to 741741

In-Game Alternatives

  • Try playing ARAM or Co-Op vs AI to ease competitive pressure
  • Mute toxic players early – protect your energy
  • Take breaks after every match – stretch, hydrate, breathe

Offline Self-Care

  • Meditation Apps: Headspace, Calm
  • Journaling your tilt moments – recognize patterns
  • Physical activity – even a short walk helps reset

Choose the path that keeps your mind at peace – not just your LP intact.
