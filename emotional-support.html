Emotional Support

If League of Legends is starting to take a toll on your emotions, you're not alone. It's okay to take breaks, ask for help, and talk about how you feel.

Ways to Take Care of Yourself

  • Set Limits: Try not to play for long stretches. Use timers or schedules to help you balance gaming and other responsibilities.
  • Talk to Friends: Connecting with teammates or friends can be a great way to vent or get encouragement.
  • Step Away When Tilted: If you’re frustrated or upset, it’s okay to log off. Breathe. Stretch. Walk.
  • Practice Self-Compassion: You’re not defined by your rank. Be kind to yourself—mistakes are part of learning.
  • Reach Out: If things feel overwhelming, don’t hesitate to speak to a counselor or someone you trust.

You matter more than any match. Be kind to your mind, Summoner.
